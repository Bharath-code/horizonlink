## Revenue Playbook Overview

### 1. Direct-to-Consumer (B2C)
- **Offering:** Premium household subscription ($69/yr or $6.99/mo) plus add-ons (extended history, caregiver reports).
- **Target:** Busy dual-income households, caregivers, remote workers.
- **Acquisition:** Paid social, content/SEO, referrals, app store search.
- **Economics:**
  - CAC: ~$18 blended (paid + organic) after optimization.
  - Conversion: 8–12% of active free users to paid within 60 days.
  - Churn: 3.5% monthly with habit loops (TV widgets, daily summaries).
  - LTV: ~$180 (ARPA $7.5/mo × 24-month lifespan × 30% take rate after stores/fees).
  - Gross margin: ~55% after infra/support.
  - ARR goal: 50K paying households → ~$4.2M ARR; net contribution ≈ $2.3M.

### 2. Business-to-Business (B2B)
- **Offering:** HorizonLink Care Console — multi-seat dashboard, compliance exports, API access, SLA support.
- **Verticals:** Senior living facilities, home-health agencies, field services, last-mile fleets.
- **Packaging & Pricing:**
  - Starter: $99/mo (20 monitored riders/clients) + $4 per additional seat.
  - Growth: $299/mo (100 seats) + geofence automation + priority support.
  - Enterprise: Custom; SSO, HIPAA Business Associate Agreements, dedicated success manager.
- **Sales Motion:** Outbound founder-led, LinkedIn drip, industry conferences, channel partners.
- **Economics:**
  - CAC: ~$450 (AE time + events) with 5–6 month payback.
  - Gross margin: 65% (higher ARPU covers infra + support).
  - 500 Starter plans ≈ $594K ARR; mix of Growth/Enterprise lifts B2B ARR to $1.2M in year 3.
  - Infra cost incremental: ~$8/customer/mo (maps + push at scale) → manageable under 20% COGS.

### 3. Business-to-Business-to-Consumer (B2B2C)
- **Offering:** White-label or co-branded app for mobility fleets, gig platforms, concierge services; includes templated TV dashboards and voice skills.
- **Revenue Model:** Platform fee ($2K setup + $499/mo) + revenue share on downstream consumer upgrades (20% of subscription purchased by end users).
- **Pipeline:** Airport shuttle operators, school bus dispatchers, premium rideshare startups.
- **Economics:**
  - CAC: ~$1,200 (solution engineer + legal) but large ACV ($8K–$12K/yr) → payback < 3 months.
  - TAM slice: 30K US fleets with connected operations → $300M serviceable.
  - SOM goal: 150 partners in 3 years → ~$1.5M platform fees + $800K share on upsells.

## Total Addressable Market & Segmentation
- **Family location safety TAM:** ~$5.3B (global) with 15% share accessible to cross-device experiences.
- **Home-health & elder care compliance TAM:** ~$2.6B (North America) for real-time visit verification.
- **Fleet/concierge mobility TAM:** ~$1.8B in SaaS spend (dispatch + visibility) where HorizonLink can bolt on trust features.
- Combined opportunity > $9B; initial Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM) targeted at ~$4M ARR within 3 years through combined motions.

## Infrastructure & Operating Costs (Year 3 Steady State)
- Cloud compute/storage: ~$42K/yr (microservices, databases, CDN).
- Maps/ETA APIs: ~$220K/yr (assumes 60M monthly route calls; negotiated enterprise plan).
- Messaging/push/WebSockets: ~$36K/yr (FCM + Pub/Sub + fallback SMS credits).
- Support & moderation: ~$90K/yr (contract staff + tooling).
- Total COGS: ~$388K/yr → supports ~55–60% gross margin overall.

## Path to $1M Founder Take-Home
1. **Lock 15K annual prepay households**
   - Focus on $69/yr plan with “founder lifetime rate” to pull cash forward.
   - At 15K subs → $1.035M cash receipts; after 55% gross margin and lean ops ($350K spend), founder can draw ~$200K and reinvest remainder.
2. **Layer B2B/B2B2C high-ACV deals**
   - 200 Starter B2B clients ($99/mo) + 50 Growth ($299/mo) → ~$412K ARR.
   - 25 B2B2C partners at $6K ACV average → ~$150K ARR + consumer revenue share (~$120K).
3. **Combined Contribution**
   - B2C net: ~$2.3M gross profit.
   - B2B/B2B2C net: ~$650K gross profit (after 35% costs).
   - Operating expenses (product, marketing, G&A) capped at ~$1.6M keeps net profit near $1.35M → allows $1M founder distribution while sustaining growth.

## Differentiation Playbook
- **Device Coverage:** Only platform bundling Android phone sharing + Android/Fire TV + voice assistants out of the box.
- **Automation Depth:** Advanced schedules, geofence-based smart alerts, compliance exports for agencies, API hooks for fleets.
- **Privacy & Trust:** Clear consent ledger, family governance features, upcoming SOC2 compliance, optional on-prem storage for enterprise.
- **Partnership Hooks:** Co-brand modules, affiliate revenue splits, and integration kits for care/fleet software drive stickiness competitors lack.
- **Premium Experience:** High-conversion landing pages, concierge onboarding for annual plans, and continuous behavioral nudges to keep retention high.
